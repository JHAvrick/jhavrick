They say if you stand in front of a mirror in a dark room and whisper ["developers, developers, developers"](https://www.bing.com/videos/riverview/relatedvideo?&q=developers+developers+developers&&mid=7A55BAD6B4659CBD983D7A55BAD6B4659CBD983D&&FORM=VRDGAR) you can summon the ghost of XP-era [Steve Ballmer](https://media.vanityfair.com/photos/54ca8f90f547ce3c06e1c9b9/master/pass/image.jpg). Anyways, if you'd like to get in touch with me you can try uttering my name thrice into a mirror or you can just use this blog's [contact form](/contact), email me at josh@avrick.dev (preferred), or send me a message on [LinkedIn](https://www.linkedin.com/in/joshavrick/).

In the meantime, feel free to take a look around my [Github](https://github.com/JHAvrick) or enjoy some selected photos below from recent travels, all shot on a [Fuji XT-4](https://fujifilm-x.com/en-us/products/cameras/x-t4/) using an 18-55mm lens.
